Tarsier

Tarsiers are capable of turning their heads nearly 180° in each direction, allowing them the ability to rotate their heads almost 360°

  • Scientefic Name : Tarsiidae
  • Average Length : 9 to 16 cm
  • Average Lifespan : 2 to 12 Years
  • Scientefic Name : Rain Forests

The tarsier is a tiny, nocturnal primate known for its huge eyes and powerful jumping abilities. Native to Southeast Asia, it feeds mainly on insects and can leap great distances between trees, using its long fingers and strong hind legs.
